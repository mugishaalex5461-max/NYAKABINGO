About Our School

Building Excellence Through Education

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To provide quality primary education that prepares pupils for secondary education and develops responsible, knowledgeable, and skills-oriented citizens of Uganda.

Vision: To be a leading primary school in Kisoro District, known for academic excellence, discipline, and holistic development of our learners.

Our Facilities

Nyakabingo Primary School is equipped with essential learning facilities to support quality education:

  • Adequate Classrooms - Well-ventilated and spacious learning spaces
  • Staff Room - Dedicated space for teachers to plan and prepare lessons
  • Administrative Office - For academic records and pupil management
  • Sports Grounds - For physical education and athletic activities
  • Playgrounds - Safe spaces for recreational activities
  • Water & Sanitation - Adequate facilities for pupil health and hygiene

Government Support

As a government-aided school, Nyakabingo Primary School operates under the Ministry of Education's guidelines and standards. We receive government support for teachers' salaries and operational expenses, which allows us to provide affordable primary education to children in our community. Our commitment to the national curriculum and education standards ensures that our graduates are well-prepared for further education.
